About

What is PetPouch?

PetPouch is a pet health tracking app for iPhone, built by a cat dad in Seattle who couldn't find anything made with real care for pet owners. It helps you keep track of everything important about your pets — from health issues, allergies and medications to vet appointments, meal schedules and vaccinations.

At a glance, the My Pets tab shows a live summary of each pet's next actions — upcoming appointments, medication times and meal reminders. Tap any pet to see their full health summary, with colour-coded status cards for allergies, health issues, medications, vaccinations, meal plan and weight. Every section is just one tap away.

PetPouch is free to download with a generous free tier, and offers a Premium subscription for unlimited pets, reminders, advanced features and more.

Getting started

How do I add my first pet?

Tap the My Pets tab at the bottom of the screen, then tap the + button in the top right corner. Enter your pet's name, species, breed, birthday, photo and other optional information. Tap Save and your pet will appear on the My Pets screen.

Food

What's the difference between Schedule and Log?

Schedule is your pet's regular meal plan — the foods you normally give them, with meal times and portion sizes. Think of it as a template for what you plan to feed.

Log (Premium) is a daily record of what you actually fed your pet. You can log feedings, record how your pet responded, and track actual calories against your target. The Log tab is optional — many users get great value from the Schedule alone.

Premium

What's included in the free version vs Premium?

Free includes: 1 pet, 1 health issue, 1 allergy, 1 medication, 1 vaccination · Unlimited vet appointments · At-a-glance My Pets view with next actions · Pet health summary with colour-coded status cards · Pet notes · Meal schedule · CSV export and more.

Premium includes: Unlimited pets, health records and tracking · Weight tracking with charts · Advanced calendar view · Reminders for medications, meals, vaccinations and birthdays · Food log with calorie tracking · PDF export · Pet profile card sharing.

Premium is available as a monthly ($2.99/mo) or annual ($19.99/yr) subscription.

Subscriptions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your PetPouch Premium subscription at any time through your iPhone Settings. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions, find PetPouch, and tap to manage or cancel.

Cancelling will stop future charges. You'll retain Premium access until the end of your current billing period.

Notifications

How do I enable reminders?

Reminders are a Premium feature. Once you've upgraded, go to the Settings tab (gear icon on the My Pets screen) and toggle on the reminder types you'd like — medications, meals, vaccinations and birthdays.

If PetPouch doesn't have notification permission, you'll be prompted to enable it in your iPhone Settings.

Data

Is my pet data backed up?

All pet data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your iPhone, your PetPouch data will be included in your device backup and will restore when you set up a new iPhone.

You can also export your data as a CSV file from the Settings tab at any time.

Data

What happens to my data if I downgrade from Premium?

Nothing is deleted. All your existing records remain fully visible and editable. The only change is that you won't be able to add new records beyond the free tier limits. Your data is always yours.

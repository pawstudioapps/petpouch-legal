Overview

Your data stays on your device

PetPouch stores all pet information locally on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data or your pets' data with third parties. We have no servers. We have no database of your pets.

What we store

Information you enter

Pet names, photos, health records, vet appointments, medications, vaccinations, weight entries, allergies, and favorite foods are stored only on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it yourself using the CSV or PDF export feature.

Photos

Pet photos

Pet photos you add are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared. When you share a Pet Profile Card, you are sharing an image generated on your device — we play no role in that process.

Notifications

Push notifications

If you enable notifications, these are processed entirely on your device by Apple's notification system. We do not have access to notification content, delivery status, or any related data.

Analytics

No tracking or analytics

PetPouch does not collect usage analytics, crash reports, or any diagnostic data. We do not use any analytics SDKs or third-party tracking libraries. PetPouch products are ad-free and we do not allow advertisers to promote their products or services through PetPouch.

Third parties

No third-party integrations

PetPouch does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The only external service used is Apple's MapKit for vet clinic search, which is subject to Apple's own privacy policy.

Children

Suitable for all ages

PetPouch is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

Policy updates

If we update this policy, the new version will appear in the app and on this page. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when changes are made.
